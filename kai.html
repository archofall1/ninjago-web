Biography & Lore

Originally a blacksmith working at his father's shop, "Four Weapons," Kai was the last of the original four ninja to be recruited by Wu. His journey began when the Skeleton Army kidnapped his sister, Nya.

Kai is known for his "act first, think later" attitude. While he once struggled with the fact that he wasn't the "Green Ninja," he eventually discovered that his true role was to protect the one who was.
