Biography & Lore

Master Chen was the owner of "Chen's Noodle House" and the leader of a secret cult. He orchestrated the Tournament of Elements to steal the elemental powers of the ninja and their allies.

His ultimate goal was to use a spell to transform himself and his followers into Anacondrai warriors to restart the Serpentine War and conquer Ninjago once and for all.
