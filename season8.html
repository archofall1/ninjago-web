The Story

Ninjago City has changed, and a new threat has risen: a mysterious motorcycle gang called the Sons of Garmadon. They are obsessed with a dark ritual involving three Oni Masks that will allow them to resurrect Lord Garmadon—but only his most evil, destructive side. Lloyd, now a young man and leader of the team, falls deeply in love with Princess Harumi, the 'Jade Princess' of Ninjago.

In a brutal betrayal, Harumi reveals she is 'The Quiet One,' the leader of the gang. She blames the Ninja for the death of her parents during the Great Devourer’s attack years ago. Despite the Ninja’s best efforts, the ritual succeeds. Lord Garmadon is brought back as a cold, heartless monster of destruction. He summons a massive stone Colossus that crushes the Ninja's destiny, seemingly killing them and leaving Lloyd powerless and alone in a conquered city.
