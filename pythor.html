Biography & Lore

Pythor is the last surviving member of the Anacondrai tribe. After being released from the Tomb of the Anacondrai by Lloyd, he betrayed the young boy to unite the Serpentine tribes and summon the Great Devourer.

He is a master manipulator who has returned multiple times to plague the ninja, often working from the shadows or aligning himself with greater powers like the Overlord to achieve his goals.
