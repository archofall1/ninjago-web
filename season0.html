The Story

Before the Great Devourer, before the Overlord, and before the Green Ninja, there was a simple blacksmith named Kai. His life is turned upside down when his sister, Nya, is kidnapped by the Skulkin Army led by the terrifying Samukai. In his moment of desperation, an old master named Wu appears, offering to train Kai in the ancient art of Spinjitzu so he can save his sister and protect the world from a rising darkness.

Kai soon discovers he is not alone. He joins forces with three other students: Jay, the Master of Lightning; Cole, the Master of Earth; and Zane, the Master of Ice. Together, they embark on a dangerous quest across Ninjago to recover the Four Golden Weapons—the Scythe of Quakes, the Shurikens of Ice, the Nunchucks of Lightning, and the Sword of Fire—before the Skulkin can deliver them to the Underworld.

The journey takes them to the Fire Temple, where Kai is forced to choose between the Sword of Fire and saving his sister. In the Underworld, they face a betrayal by Samukai, who attempts to wield all four weapons at once, only to be consumed by their power. This opens a portal for Lord Garmadon to escape into another realm. The Pilot Season ends with the four Ninja standing tall as the new protectors of Ninjago, unaware of the even greater threats that destiny has in store for them.
