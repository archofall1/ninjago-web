SEASON 10

March of the Oni

← Back to Main Page
Main Villain: The Omega / The Oni
Key Event: The Tornado of Creation
Alliance: Ninja & Garmadon Team-up

The Story

The ultimate darkness has finally arrived. The Oni—the ancient ancestors of Garmadon—enter Ninjago through the Realm Crystal, bringing a 'Black Cloud' that turns anyone it touches into stone. This threat is so great that Lloyd is forced to do the unthinkable: release Lord Garmadon from prison to help them fight a foe that only an Oni can understand.

The Ninja retreat to the Monastery of Spinjitzu for a final stand. As the cloud surrounds them, they realize that their individual powers aren't enough to stop the void. In a callback to the very beginning of the series, the Ninja, Wu, and Garmadon join hands to perform the 'Tornado of Creation.' The resulting blast of pure energy wipes the Oni from existence. In the aftermath, Lloyd has a vision of the First Spinjitzu Master, who thanks him for his service and offers him a choice: stay in the afterlife or return to his friends.
