Biography & Lore

Primary Interactive X-ternal Assistant Life-form. Originally Zane's assistant, she sacrificed her physical body to save him before building herself a new one and becoming Samurai X.

P.I.X.A.L. provides technical support and combat backup. She has no elemental power, but her advanced AI and weaponry make her just as dangerous as any ninja.
