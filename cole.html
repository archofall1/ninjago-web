Biography & Lore

The son of a famous dancer, Cole initially went to the Marty Forth Performing Arts Academy before realizing his true path was that of a warrior. He was the first ninja recruited by Wu.

Cole provides the physical and emotional strength of the team. Even after being turned into a ghost for a period of time, he remained a vital member of the team, eventually gaining his "Burst" abilities.
