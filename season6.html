The Story

After the destruction of the Cursed Realm, a teapot containing the wicked Djinn, Nadakhan, is found. Nadakhan is a pirate captain with the power to grant three wishes—but he always twists them to cause misery. He begins a campaign of psychological warfare, tricking the Ninja into wishing themselves away into his 'Djinn Blade' so he can use their energy to rebuild his fallen realm of Djinjago in the sky.

Jay becomes the last Ninja standing and must lead a ragtag crew of 'Substitute Ninja' to infiltrate Nadakhan’s flying fortress. The stakes become personal as Nadakhan plans to marry Nya to gain infinite wishes. In a race against time, Jay is forced to use his final wish to undo the entire season's events. He wishes that the teapot was never found, erasing the timeline and leaving only him and Nya with the memories of their love and the battle in the clouds.
