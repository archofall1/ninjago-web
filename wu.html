Biography & Lore

The son of the First Spinjitzu Master and the younger brother of Garmadon. Wu has lived for centuries, guarding the secrets of Ninjago and training heroes to defend it.

He often speaks in riddles and expects his students to find their own path. His wisdom is the light that guides the ninja through their darkest battles.
