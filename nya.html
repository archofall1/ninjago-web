Biography & Lore

Kai’s sister, Nya, refused to stay on the sidelines. She first fought as the mysterious Samurai X before discovering she had inherited her mother's power over water.

She is an expert mechanic and a fierce warrior who balances her technological skills with her elemental mastery. Nya is the heart and soul of the ninja team.
