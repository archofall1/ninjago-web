Biography & Lore

The son of a legendary dancer, Cole chose the path of a ninja instead of following in his father's footsteps. He was the very first ninja recruited by Master Wu.

As the Master of Earth, Cole provides the physical strength for the team. He is dependable and grounded, though his legendary appetite for chocolate cake is often his only weakness.
