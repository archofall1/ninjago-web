Biography & Lore

Zane is a Nindroid (ninja robot) created by Dr. Julien to protect those who cannot protect themselves. For a long time, he didn't realize he was a robot, simply thinking he was "different."

He is the team's navigator and logic-processor. Zane often sacrifices himself for his friends, proving that you don't need a heart of flesh to have a soul of gold.
